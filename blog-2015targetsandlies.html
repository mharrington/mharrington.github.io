2015 targets and lies

So this might be a little early to be predicting how my running will go in 2015. However, I wanted to put my targets somewhere early so I am honest with myself. When setting targets for my running I find that i'm not a very honest person. I tend to change or reconsider what my target was after my run depending how it has gone. Now, you might thing this might mean I always say "Ah, I wasn't really aiming for a PB". But I also shift my targets when I do better than planned. For example "Ah, why didn't I push a little harder to break X minutes" rather than being "Woop! I've met my target".
