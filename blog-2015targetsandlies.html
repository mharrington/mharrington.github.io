2015 targets and lies

So this might be a little early to be predicting how my running will go in 2015. However, I wanted to put my targets somewhere early so I am honest with myself. When setting targets for my running I find that I lie. I tend to change or reconsider what my target was after my run depending how it has gone. Now, you might thing this might mean I always say "Ah, I wasn't really aiming for a PB". But it's not always like that. I also shift my targets when I do better than planned. For example "Ah, why didn't I push a little harder to break X minutes" rather than being "Woop! I've met my target". So this year i'm putting them somewhere public so I can look back and really see how i've done.

Below are my current best times

Southend 10km 40:03

I'm definitely not running another marathon this year, so I wont be improving that time, but I would like to improve all my others. So with that in mind, my targets for 2015 are:

2015 targets

5km 18:30 and below
10km 39:00 and below

The biggest stretch is definitely for my half marathon time. Taking 22 minutes off my pb seems huge now, but after nearly getting sub 40 for 10km this year I think I can do it. Anyway, now it's written down, at least I can't go changing my mind!
