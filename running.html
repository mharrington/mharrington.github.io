something about running here

I've been running since about 2009. I started running to lose wait and then got the bug. After running for a year or so I transitioned to barefoot and haven't looked back since.

I gave barefoot running a chance after reading Born to Run by Christopher McDougall. I read that book and was taken in by the whole idea and just had to give it a go. I purchased a pair of Vibram KSOs from Ebay, went out for a run around the block and was hooked. Since then i've run 5kms, 10kms, half marathons, marathons and off-road races. Running barefoot just makes me happy.

I now run regularly and wouldn't have it any other way. Below are my pbs across the different distances I have run.
